Oatmeal

  1. 1 packet of brown sugar Quick Quaker oatmeal
  2. 1 spoon
  3. 1 bowl
  4. 3 cups of water
  5. 1 stick of butter
  6. 1 Teaspoon of milk
Step 1
Pour packets into bowl
Step 2
Pour 3 cups of water into the bowl
Step 3
Microwave for 2:50 minutes
Step 4
Take out of microwave and put one slice of butter to melt into oatmeal
Step 5
Pour teaspoon of milk into oatmeal
Step 6
Mix melted butter, milk, and oatmeal to perfection
